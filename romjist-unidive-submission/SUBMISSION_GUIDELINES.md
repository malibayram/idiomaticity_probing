## Hard requirements

- **10–12 pages**, mandatory **ROMJIST LaTeX template** (final versions LaTeX-only since 1 Sep 2024).
- Partial / preliminary results are **not accepted** — present a complete study.
- Original work, not under review elsewhere.
- **Each author may appear on only one paper** in the special issue (affects co-author list).
- Papers with hallucinated references are **desk-rejected** — verify every citation.
- An **AI-use disclosure statement** is required if AI tools assisted writing (allowed for
  language/readability only, not for producing insights or conclusions).
- Submit by email with: the paper (Word or PDF for review), a signed **Cover Letter**
  (includes Statement of Originality), and the signed **Copyright Form**.
